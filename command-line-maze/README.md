# Global Kitchen Quest  
**Created by: Flynn Huynh**

## About This Maze
Embark on a culinary adventure around the world to discover a legendary street food
dish that represents the ultimate fusion of flavor, culture, and tradition!

## DISCLAIMER:
***This is just for entertaining purposes, the final dish isn't my favorite dish but is very cultural important in my opinion.***

## Theme
This maze takes you through different world cuisines and their iconic dishes, 
from Italian pizzerias to Vietnamese food stalls to Mexican cantinas.

## Instructions
1. Start at the `restaurant_entrance` directory
2. Please read the `menu.txt` before ordering
3. Navigate through different cuisines and kitchens
4. Read about iconic dishes and follow the clues
5. Avoid tourist traps and dead ends!
6. Discover hidden secrets from traditional cooks
7. **READ EVERYTHING (All .txt file)!!**

## Essential Commands
- `ls` - see what's in the current location
- `ls -a` - reveal EVERYTHING (including hidden family secrets!)
- `cd [directory_name]` - enter a kitchen/restaurant
- `cd ..` - go back to the previous location
- `cat [filename]` - read menus, recipes, and clues
- `pwd` - check where you are in the world

## Hints
- Not every restaurant is authentic - watch out for tourist traps!
- Traditional cooks often have hidden secrets (files starting with '.')
- Follow the clues in order - they'll guide you through different cuisines
- The best dishes often come from cultural fusion!

## Goal
Find the legendary "La Comida Perfecta" and learn what makes it so special!
